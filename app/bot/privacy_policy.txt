PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how Telegram Gmail Bot ("we", "our", or "the Service") 
collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Telegram bot to access 
your Gmail account.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Google Account Information
- Email address associated with your Google account
- OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens) for Gmail API access

2.2 Telegram Information
- Telegram user ID
- Telegram username (if available)
- First and last name (if available)
- Language preference

2.3 Email Data
- We access your emails in read-only mode
- Email content is processed temporarily to display in Telegram
- We do not store email content permanently

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate you with Google Gmail API
- Display your emails within Telegram
- Detect and highlight verification codes (2FA)
- Send notifications about new emails (if enabled)

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Token Storage
- OAuth tokens are encrypted using industry-standard encryption (Fernet/AES)
- Tokens are stored in a secure database
- We never store your Google password

4.2 Email Content
- Email content is fetched on-demand and displayed in Telegram
- We do not permanently store email content on our servers
- Email data is transmitted securely via HTTPS

5. DATA SHARING

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Access your emails for any purpose other than displaying them to you

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Disconnect your Gmail account at any time via /settings command
- Request deletion of all your data
- Revoke access through Google Account settings (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)

7. DATA RETENTION

- Your OAuth tokens are stored until you disconnect your account
- When you disconnect an account, all associated tokens are deleted
- Telegram user data is retained while you use the service

8. GOOGLE API SERVICES USER DATA POLICY

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the 
Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We only request the minimum necessary scope:
- gmail.readonly - Read-only access to your emails

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. 
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. 
We will notify users of any material changes via the bot.
